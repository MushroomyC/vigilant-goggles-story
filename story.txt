Allen only felt exhausted after his struggle to leave the bed. "I only took two
hours of sleep," he thought, brushing his teeth up and down like a robot," and
I still got an exam today, with another essay to hand in." He took a look at 
his watch. The exam will begin within only forty minutes, which means no time
for breakfast.
He grabbed his backpack and left his dorm. The air outside was frigid, too bad 
he forgot his hat upstairs. "Oh well," he thought and took a left down the road
and started towards his exam. With each step he took, he could feel the nerves
starting to creep up. "I sure hope I didn't forget anything," he thought one last
time as his dorm was beginning to leave his sight.
